The Texarkana Magical Realism Fictional Book Prize

A literary prize awarded each year for the best original novel that includes magical elements in an otherwise mundane or realistic environment. The prize may serve to crown a lifetime’s achievement or to direct attention to an important body of work that is still developing. The prize is not open to application.

Past Winners

The T.M.R.F. Book Prize committee diligently reads all fictional books released throughout the year and comes to a consensus on who should win the grand prize.
